# ModelMate Terms and Acceptable Use Draft

This document is an MVP-stage terms and acceptable use draft, not a final legal
terms of service document.

## Service Role

ModelMate provides predictive analysis assistance. It does not guarantee
accurate, fair, or production-ready decisions.

ModelMate는 예측 분석을 보조하는 도구이며, 정확성·공정성·운영 적합성을
보장하지 않습니다.

## Appropriate Use

ModelMate helps users inspect CSV data, compare predictive models, review
evidence, and generate reports. Results should be treated as decision-support
material, not as a final automated decision.

Do not use ModelMate as the sole basis for high-risk decisions, including:

- legal decisions
- medical diagnosis or treatment decisions
- financial eligibility decisions
- hiring, firing, promotion, or disciplinary decisions
- decisions that can significantly affect a person's rights or opportunities

## User Responsibility

Users are responsible for:

- having the right to upload and analyze the data
- removing sensitive or unnecessary personal information
- checking whether the result is appropriate for their domain
- reviewing model limitations, warnings, and evidence summaries

## Prohibited Use

Do not use ModelMate for:

- illegal activity
- discriminatory profiling or targeting
- privacy-invasive monitoring
- uploading secrets, passwords, payment data, or regulated personal data without
  appropriate permission and protection
- presenting model output as guaranteed truth

## Model Limitations

Model performance depends on data quality, target selection, leakage risk,
validation settings, and the amount of available data. A strong score in one
uploaded dataset does not guarantee future production performance.
